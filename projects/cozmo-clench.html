Cozmo Clench

Cozmo Clench is a gripping and object-clenching robot built for the national-level competition organized by Techfest IIT Bombay. The robot is designed to navigate an arena, locate specific objects, and securely grab and transport them to designated zones using a custom clenching mechanism.
