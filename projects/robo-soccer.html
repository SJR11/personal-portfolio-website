Robo Soccer

Robo Soccer is a manually controlled robot designed to play soccer in a team-based competition. It features a rugged and responsive control system, fast motors for dribbling and chasing the ball, and a front-mounted kicking mechanism. The robot is tuned for agility, coordination, and rapid direction changes, mimicking human soccer moves.
