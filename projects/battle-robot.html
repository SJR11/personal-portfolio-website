Battle Robot

This battle robot was designed and built for national and international Robo War competitions. With a reinforced chassis, high-torque motors, and an aggressive low-ground design, it is capable of performing powerful ramming, pushing, and flipping maneuvers in combat arenas. The robot is manually operated using a wireless remote system.
