Robo Sumo

Robo Sumo is a competitive robot designed to overpower and push opponents out of the arena. It is engineered with torque-rich motors, high-traction wheels, and sharp sensor response to detect and counter enemy robots quickly. This project was built for aggressive performance in sumo rings and incorporates both offensive and defensive tactics.
