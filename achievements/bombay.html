Techfest IIT Bombay – Cozmo Clench

Techfest IIT Bombay is Asia’s largest science and technology festival, attracting robotics teams from across the globe. Our team participated in the international Cozmo Clench competition, where we built a precision-controlled robot capable of gripping, navigating mazes, and completing the task course under strict timing and accuracy.

Technologies Used

  • 🤖 Robot Type: Object Clenching & Navigation
  • 🧠 Control: Arduino-based logic
  • 🛠️ Mechanism: Servo-based claw system
  • 📡 Mode: Manual navigation using wired controller
  • 🏆 Result: Top 10 International Participants
