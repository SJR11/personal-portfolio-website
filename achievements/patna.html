Celesta 2024 – IIT Patna

Celesta 2024, the annual techno-management fest of IIT Patna, hosted various national and international robotics challenges. Our robotics team participated in three major events — Robo Soccer, Robo Race, and Robo War.

Achievements

  • ⚽ Robo Soccer: Winner and 2nd Runner Up
  • 🚗 Robo Race: Reached Top 10 Finalist among national-level participants
  • ⚔️ Robo War: Competed until the Knockout Stage

Technologies Used

  • 🎮 Manual Controls: Joystick-controlled RF Robots (Soccer & War)
  • 🧠 Controllers: Arduino UNO + Motor Driver Modules
  • ⚙️ Mobility: High-torque geared motors with omni or skid steering
  • 📍 Venue: Indian Institute of Technology, Patna
